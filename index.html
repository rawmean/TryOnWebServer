Privacy & Data Protection

Your Privacy Matters

At TryOn, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Here's how we handle your data:

Image Processing

We do not retain any images or information related to the user on our servers. All image processing happens locally on your device or through secure, temporary cloud processing that is deleted after use.

Facial Features Protection

No facial features are stored on our servers. Our AI technology processes your images to create virtual try-on results without storing or retaining any biometric data or facial recognition information.

Data Security

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard security protocols. We implement strict access controls and regularly audit our systems to ensure your information remains secure.

User Control

You have complete control over your data. You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or how we handle your data, please contact us at apps@maadotaa.com
